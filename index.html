What is Tub Refinishing

Tub refinishing, also known as bathtub restoration, is the process of rejuvenating the surface of a worn-out bathtub to make it look like new again. During the refinishing process, professionals use specialized techniques and materials to repair any cracks, chips, or stains on the surface, followed by a durable, smooth coating that restores the tub�s original luster. This cost-effective service is an excellent alternative to bathtub replacement, saving you both time and money while delivering stunning results.
